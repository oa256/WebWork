HOW TO MAKE A HAM-SANDWICH

INSTURUCTIONS

  1. Step 1

    Take two slices of bread

  2. Step 2

    Toast the bread in a toaster

  3. Step 3

    Spread mayonnaise on one slice of the toast

  4. Step 4

    Place tomato slices on the bread

  5. Step 5

    Place a lot of lettuce leaves over the tomato slices

  6. Step 6

    Place a few slices of ham over lettuce

  7. Step 7

    Place a slice of your favorite cheese over the ham

  8. Step 8

    Spread mayonnaise on the second slice of toast

  9. Step 9

    Cut the sandwich
